DATS 6401 - Project 2 - Parisa Nazarijam


My plan for this project starts with reviewing and visualizing data on the global immigrants including refugees and asylum seekers based on demographics such as(population, country of birth, age, occupation, marital status),type and class of their admission (immediate relatives of U.S. citizens, family-sponsored preferences, employment-based preferences, diversity, refugees and asylum seekers). For this project, I referred to several sources such as The World Bank, UNICEF, United Nation, Homeland Security, and Migration Policy Institute. The data presents the estimates of international migrant by age, sex, and their origin from 2010 to 2017 and are available for 30 countries, which have the highest immigration rate.

Objectives:

  • Taking advantage of data visualization to grasp a more comprehensive insights of the human immigration phenomenon worldwide.
  • Focusing on the United States immigration systemin 2017 by showing the most current demographic, and social facts about immigrants.
